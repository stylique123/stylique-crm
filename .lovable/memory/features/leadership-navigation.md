name: Leadership 2-Bucket Navigation
description: CEO/COO sidebar uses 2-bucket model: Needs My Decision + Needs My Attention, with supporting pages below
type: feature
---
CEO/COO primary navigation has 3 items:
1. Command Center — summary KPI tiles + 2 bucket cards + team status + SDR performance
2. Needs My Decision — approvals, confirmations, escalations requiring a click
3. Needs My Attention — business risks, blocked items, overdue payments, absent staff

"More" section: Payments & Clients, Trials, People & Performance, Pipeline, Contacts, Directives, Calendar, Settings

Directives are NOT a dashboard category — they are an action (button in Command Center header or launched from a record). The Directives page lives in "More" for history/tracking.

Command Center top KPIs: MRR, Active Clients, Revenue at Risk.
Command Center quick links at bottom: Payments & Clients, Trials, Contacts, Directives.

People & Performance tabs: Overview, Attendance, KPI, Leave, Compensation (no separate "People" tab).
